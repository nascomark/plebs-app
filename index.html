What is Nostr?

Nostr is a decentralized social protocol that gives you ownership of your identity and content. Unlike traditional platforms, no company can ban you, censor your content, or shut down your account.

Why does Plebs use Nostr?

Plebs is built on Nostr to provide censorship-resistant video sharing. Your videos, comments, and interactions are stored across a network of relays, not on our servers. Your Nostr identity works across hundreds of other apps too!

How it works

  • Your keys = Your identity. You'll get a private key (nsec) that proves you own your account.
  • Keep your private key secret! It's like a password that can never be reset.
  • One identity, many apps. Use the same account on Damus, Primal, Amethyst, and more.

How to use your private key

1

Save it securely: Store your nsec in a password manager, write it down, or keep it in an encrypted file. Never share it publicly!

2

To login again: Click "Private Key" on the login screen and paste your nsec. It works like a password.

3

Use on other apps: Your nsec works on all Nostr apps - Damus, Primal, Amethyst, and hundreds more!

4

Consider a signer app: For better security, use apps like Amber (Android) or a browser extension like Alby to manage your keys.
